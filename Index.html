The woman who started it all:
Eileen

Founded by Eileen Massey, a nurse with almost 2 decades of experience dealing with PTSD and trauma, she made it her personal mission to ensure that those who are protected our society with everything they had are offered the care and support they need to re-acclimate themselves to civilian lives. They may leave their battlefields, but those battlefields never truly leave them.
